ospf-router/

│   └── benchmark.cpp           standalone executable — prints avg/p50/p99 reconvergence µs

│   └── test_lsdb.cpp           7 tests — LSA freshness, failLink, reconvergence correctness

│   ├── vite.config.js          dev server config (port 5173)

│   └── src/
